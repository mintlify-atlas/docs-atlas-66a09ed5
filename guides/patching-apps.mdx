---
title: "Patching apps"
description: "How to select and apply patches to an Android app using ReVanced Manager."
---

ReVanced Manager patches apps by injecting modifications directly into the APK on your device. Follow the steps below to patch your first app.

<Warning>
  Only full APK files are supported. APK bundles (split APKs) cannot be patched. If you downloaded an app from the Play Store using a tool like APK Extractor, make sure it exported a single `.apk` file.
</Warning>

## Steps to patch an app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps tab">
    Tap the **Apps** tab in the top navigation bar of ReVanced Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an app">
    Tap an app from the list of supported apps, or tap **Select from storage** to load an APK file directly from your device.

    <Note>
      Selecting from storage is protected by a safeguard. If the option is unavailable, enable the relevant safeguard under **Settings > Advanced**.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review patch selection">
    Optionally review and change the selected patches. The default selection is recommended for most users.

    <Tip>
      Tap the **Reset** button in the bottom right corner of the patch selector to restore the default patch selection. Sticking with defaults avoids compatibility issues.
    </Tip>

    <Note>
      Changing the patch selection is protected by a safeguard. Enable **Allow changing patch selection and options** under **Settings > Advanced** to unlock this.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a downloader (optional)">
    If multiple downloaders are installed, you can select a specific one to download the app. By default, all available downloaders are tried in order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Patch">
    Tap the **Patch** button to start the patching process. This may take several minutes depending on your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow on-screen instructions">
    If a patch requires additional input (such as an API key or login), ReVanced Manager will prompt you to complete the required steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the patched app">
    Once patching completes, tap **Install** to install the patched app on your device. To save the APK file instead, tap the save icon in the bottom left corner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

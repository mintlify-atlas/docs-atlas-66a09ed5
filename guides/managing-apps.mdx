---
title: "Managing apps"
description: "How to view, open, re-patch, or uninstall patched apps in ReVanced Manager."
---

The **Apps** tab shows all apps you have patched with ReVanced Manager. From here you can open, re-patch, or uninstall any patched app.

## Manage a patched app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps tab">
    Tap the **Apps** tab in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an app">
    Tap the app you want to manage. This opens the app detail screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an action">
    The following actions are available:

    - **Open** — Launch the patched app directly
    - **Re-patch** — Run the patching process again with the same or updated patches
    - **Uninstall** — Remove the patched app from your device
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The app detail screen shows which patches were applied, the app version that was patched, and whether the app is currently installed on your device.
</Info>

## Re-patching an app

Re-patching is useful when:
- A new version of the app is available and you want to patch it
- New or updated patches have been released
- You want to change your patch selection

Tap **Re-patch** on the app detail screen to start the patching flow again. Your previous patch selection will be pre-filled.

---
title: "Updating ReVanced Manager"
description: "How to check for and install updates to ReVanced Manager."
---

By default, ReVanced Manager checks for updates automatically each time it launches. When an update is available, a badge appears above the settings icon to let you know.

## Check for updates manually

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the settings icon in the top right corner of the ReVanced Manager home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Updates">
    Select **Updates** from the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure or trigger an update check">
    Tap **Check for updates** to check immediately, or adjust the automatic update behavior to match your preference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Enable **Pre-release versions** in the Updates screen to get early access to new features and bug fixes before they reach the stable channel. Pre-release builds may be less stable than official releases — use them with that in mind.
</Tip>

## After updating

Updating ReVanced Manager does not automatically update your patched apps. If the new version of ReVanced Manager ships with updated patches, you may need to re-patch your apps to pick up the latest changes. Open the **Apps** tab, select the app you want to update, and run the patching process again.

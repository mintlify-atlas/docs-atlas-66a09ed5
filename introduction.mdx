ReVanced Manager is an Android app that uses [ReVanced Patcher](https://github.com/revanced/revanced-patcher) to patch other Android apps directly on your device — no PC required.

## How patching works

1. **Select an app** — choose an app already installed on your device, or let ReVanced Manager download it for you using a downloader plugin.
2. **Choose patches** — pick from the available patches for that app. Patches modify app behavior, remove ads, unlock features, and more.
3. **Patch** — ReVanced Manager runs ReVanced Patcher on-device to apply the selected patches and produce a modified APK.
4. **Install** — install the patched APK directly from ReVanced Manager.

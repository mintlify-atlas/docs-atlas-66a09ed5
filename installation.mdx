---

<Warning>
  Only download ReVanced Manager from official sources. APKs from third-party sites may be modified and could compromise your device.
</Warning>

<Note>
  The latest official release is always available at [github.com/ReVanced/revanced-manager/releases/latest](https://github.com/ReVanced/revanced-manager/releases/latest).
</Note>

## Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the APK">
    Get the latest ReVanced Manager APK from [revanced.app/download](https://revanced.app/download) or from [GitHub releases](https://github.com/ReVanced/revanced-manager/releases/latest).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow installation from unknown sources">
    When you open the downloaded APK, Android may ask you to allow installation from unknown sources. Tap **Settings** in the prompt and enable the toggle for your browser or file manager, then go back and continue the installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the APK">
    Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation. Tap **Install** when asked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open ReVanced Manager">
    Launch ReVanced Manager from your app drawer. The onboarding screen will guide you through the initial setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

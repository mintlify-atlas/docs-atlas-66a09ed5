## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check prerequisites">
    Make sure your device runs Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher. See [Prerequisites](/prerequisites) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install ReVanced Manager">
    Download the latest APK from [revanced.app/download](https://revanced.app/download) or [GitHub releases](https://github.com/ReVanced/revanced-manager/releases/latest) and install it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an app to patch">
    Open the **Apps** tab, choose an app to patch, and select your desired patches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patch and install">
    Tap **Patch** to begin. Once complete, tap **Install** to install the patched app on your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>
